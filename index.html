Crusadia's Economic Engine

This is the complete blueprint for Crusadia's two-part system: The Business District, an open-world ecosystem for collaboration, and The Business Cohorts, the proving grounds for forging business warriors.

I. The Business District (The City Walls)

The open marketplace where alliances are forged and skills are sharpened. Entry is earned by completing the 55-Day Crusade.

II. The Business Cohorts (The Proving Grounds)

Progression is earned, not bought. These structured, time-bound campaigns are where proof is currency and excuses die at the gates.
